Preparing for the Move

JV September 29, 2010

Nope, not the PS Move :D We are moving to Oregon; the Silicon "Forest". I now realize it is harder to move when you are married :D We gonna travel 5 days by car and we still don't know if we gonna be able to fit everything in the car.
Anyway, I've been into model helicopters for awhile. I researched about them a little bit and I found out that I actually like the aerial photography. After that, I wanted to find something which is easy to fly because helicopters are not! I came across the quadrocopter which takes its name from its 4 propellers. This thing is awesome! It mixes the mechanical side of the modelling with computing. It has a processer and you can program it to your own use. You can find open source codes to add features like; gps, make your quad follow you, keep itself floating in the air, etc. And its main purpose is to do aerial photography. It can carry more weight than the helicopters but it doesn't mean they can't do aerobatics. They fly like, well, flies. Quads can be bought as a complete kit but it is costly. They cost about $1200, that's why the fans are buying the parts from different makers and thus lowering the cost. There are thousands of designs online and codes. There are too many things to do after moving but I want to have a quadrocopter myself. Here are some videos that got me into this hobby...
